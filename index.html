About Me

Welcome! My name is Rebecca Lyn Renavitz and I am a student at Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Anthropology from University of Delaware in 2007 and completed Master's coursework in Epidemiology at The State University of New York-University at Albany. After working at the New York State Department of Health performing data analysis, I decided to move back to New Jersey. I became interested in web development after working on a project to revamp my current company's website. It was a lot of fun, so I experimented on my own and...here I am!


On a personal note, I love ALL animals, but my all-time favourite are hairless rats...yes, hairless rats! They make excellent pets are are just so adorable! My favourite television show is The Golden Girls and I still watch re-runs ALL the time. If I could do anything else with my life, I would probably be living on a farm or animal sanctuary. Cheers!
